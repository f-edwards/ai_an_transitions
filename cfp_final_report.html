Mechanisms of AIAN Inequality in Child Welfare Across U.S. States.

Frank Edwards and Theresa Rocha Beardall

12/2020

Abstract

American Indian and Alaska Native children are separated from their families by state child welfare agencies at exceptionally high rates. This study connects contemporary trends in Native family separation to histories of Indian child removal, and locates places and institutional sites where inequality emerges.
